My business name was given to me by friends and family without them even knowing it.  In my early years, I made photo albums & miniature decorated Christmas trees as gifts.  I taught myself to sew, took up quilting and learned how to create stained glass art,but I was always drawn to creating jewelry.  I started with simple seed beads.  When you visit my photo gallery, you’ll see that my jewelry has evolved into more complex & artistic jewelry pieces utilizing various metals, silverware, vinyl albums, cork and sometimes t-shirts! Working through college and becoming a teacher, money was always tight, so friends & family often received handmade gifts and I would frequently hear the statement, “You made this?!”  After playing with those words, I had a business name.

I’m Amy!
