What does Ready to Ship mean when I track my package?

If you check the delivery online and it reads “READY to SHIP”, that means it will be shipped the next business day. For example...you purchase your item at 3 pm, I input the shipping information into the system at that time. If you happen to check the status soon after, it will read that it’s ready to ship, however, the package won’t actually get onto the USPS trucks until morning and that’s ONLY if I get to the post office that night. Remember, it may take me up to 2 days to create your order and then some more time to get it to the post office.


Packaging Material I Use

I often recycle packaging materials!!! If your item(s) arrive in a recycled box, please don’t get worried. Your item is brand new, and made by me. I personally like to recycle things as much as possible, therefor I reuse boxes, peanuts and bubble wrap.


Shipping Charges

I do not charge for the time it takes to package your items or the fuel I use to pick up boxes and/or drive your package to the post office. I am not trying to make money off of the shipping charges. If your package(s) weighs less than I quoted for shipping and there is a large overage you paid in shipping, I will absolutely issue a refund for the overpricing. But I have done my best to estimate the appropriate weight and size of the box for shipping, so please do not ask for a refund of shipping charges after the fact.
